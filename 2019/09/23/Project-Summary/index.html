Project Summary

By Yuezhou Zhao; Published on 2019-09-23

In top10 department by total payments for full time and the percentage of total payments, we find that Police, Water and Power (DWP), and Fire are the three most important parts. So the government should pay more attention on the budget of these three departments.

Over years, we can see that the overtime pay has more percentage and this trend reveals that in recent years, people have more pressure in their work and they often have to work overtime to finish their tasks.

According to the overtime pay analysis, the fire department gains the most payments. And within the firefighter, firefighter III has many more outliers than administrative analysis, which means that firefighters spend much more time doing overtime work. Thus, it’s better for the government to allocate the firefighters effectively to ensure that each firefighter and share the work and pressure.

In the average benefits cost by benefits plan over years, the DWP has the maximum rate of growth as well as the greatest average cost. So the government should control the budget pressure on the DWP department.

In the bonus VS. % bonus graph, we can see that there is the positive relationship between the total bonus and the bonus/ total payment, which means that the person who earns more money will have much more payments. I think this is not beneficial to the motivation mechanism for employees. So I think it’s better for the government to adjust the motivation system in order to achieve the stable linear correlation between the total bonus and the bonus/ total payment.
